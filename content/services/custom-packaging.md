# Custom Packaging

## Packaging That Sells

First impressions matter. **ToolsD** offers custom packaging services to help your products stand out on retail shelves, Amazon listings, or wholesale catalogs.

## Packaging Options

### Branded Retail Boxes
- Full-color printed cardboard boxes
- Your logo, product image, and specifications
- Custom sizes to fit your product perfectly

### FBA-Ready Packaging
- Amazon FNSKU barcode labels
- Suffocation warning labels (for poly bags)
- Carton labels with Amazon shipment ID
- Bundled multi-packs with proper sealing

### Industrial & Bulk Packaging
- Heavy-duty export cartons (5-layer corrugated)
- Pallet wrapping and stretch film
- Moisture-absorbent bags for sea freight
- Custom inner dividers and foam inserts

### Point-of-Sale Packaging
- Blister cards with hanging holes
- Color box with window display
- Tool roll pouches with printed logo
- Plastic carry cases with custom inserts

## Packaging Process

1. **Design Confirmation** — Share your artwork or we design for you
2. **Sample Box** — Produce one sample for approval
3. **Mass Production** — 7–15 days depending on complexity
4. **Product Packing** — We pack your products into the custom packaging
