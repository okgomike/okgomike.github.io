# Quality Inspection

## Inspect Before You Ship

Every order placed through **ToolsD** can include a pre-shipment quality inspection. We check your products at our Yiwu warehouse before they are packed for export, giving you confidence that what you ordered is exactly what you'll receive.

## What We Check

### Product Specifications
- Dimensions and sizes match your order
- Material grade and hardness verified
- Surface finish (chrome, polish, satin) inspected
- Weight and carton quantities confirmed

### Functionality Testing
- Ratchet mechanisms tested for smooth operation
- Socket fit and tolerance checked
- Wrench opening accuracy verified
- Cutting edges and tool tips inspected

### Packaging & Labeling
- Carton strength and sealing quality
- Barcode readability and accuracy
- Label placement and information correctness
- Inner packing (blister, pouch, foam) condition

### Quantity Verification
- SKU-level count against packing list
- Carton quantity and weight cross-check
- Random sampling for large orders

## Inspection Reporting

After inspection, you receive:
- **Photo report** — Images of sampled items and packaging
- **Checklist summary** — Pass/fail for each inspection point
- **Recommendation** — Ship, rework, or replace decision
