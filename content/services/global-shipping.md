# Global Shipping

## Delivered to Your Door, Anywhere

**ToolsD** ships hardware and tool orders to over 100 countries. Whether you need a small sample by express or a full container by sea, we arrange the right shipping solution for your budget and timeline.

## Shipping Methods

### Express Courier (3–7 Days)
- **DHL, FedEx, UPS**
- Best for: Samples, urgent orders under 100 kg
- Tracking: Door-to-door with online tracking
- Cost: Highest per kg, fastest delivery

### Air Freight (7–12 Days)
- Airport-to-airport or door-to-airport
- Best for: Medium orders (100–500 kg)
- Tracking: Air waybill tracking
- Cost: Mid-range, good balance of speed and price

### Sea Freight (20–40 Days)
- **LCL** (Less than Container Load) or **FCL** (Full Container)
- Best for: Bulk orders over 500 kg or 2 CBM
- Tracking: Container/BOL tracking
- Cost: Lowest per kg, best for large volumes
