# Mixed Wholesale Orders

## One Order, Multiple Products

At **ToolsD**, you don't need to order 500+ pieces of a single item to get wholesale pricing. Our mixed-order system lets you combine different products and categories into one consolidated shipment.

## How It Works

1. **Browse & Select** — Add any products from our catalog to your inquiry list.
2. **Submit Inquiry** — Send us your product list via WhatsApp or email.
3. **Receive Quote** — We consolidate pricing and shipping for your entire order.
4. **Confirm & Pay** — After confirmation, we prepare and ship everything together.

## Benefits

- **Low MOQ per SKU** — Start from 50 pcs per item
- **Category mixing** — Combine fasteners, tools, locks, and lifting equipment
- **Single shipping cost** — One freight charge for your entire order
- **Faster restocking** — Test multiple products without large inventory risk
