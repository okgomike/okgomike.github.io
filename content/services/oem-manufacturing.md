# OEM Manufacturing

## Your Brand, Our Quality

**ToolsD** works directly with Yiwu-area factories to offer OEM and private-label manufacturing for hardware, tools, and related products. Whether you need your logo on a socket set or a fully custom tool configuration, we can make it happen.

## OEM Services Available

### Laser Engraving & Logo Printing
- Ratchet handles, wrench bodies, and case lids
- Permanent, wear-resistant marking
- MOQ: 100–200 sets depending on product

### Custom Colors & Finishes
- Blow-molded case colors (red, blue, yellow, black, custom)
- Handle grip colors and surface finishes
- Powder coating and plating options

### Custom Set Configurations
- Add or remove specific socket sizes
- Combine metric and SAE in one set
- Include custom accessories (extension bars, adapters, etc.)

### Branded Packaging
- Custom-printed boxes with your logo
- Barcode and FNSKU labels for Amazon FBA
- Multi-language instruction manuals

## OEM Process

1. **Send Requirements** — Product, quantity, customization details
2. **Sample Production** — We produce a sample for your approval
3. **Mass Production** — 15–30 days depending on order size
4. **Quality Check & Ship** — Inspection before dispatch
