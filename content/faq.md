# Frequently Asked Questions

## Ordering & MOQ

**Q: What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?**
A: MOQ varies by product, typically starting from 50 pieces per SKU for standard items. Mixed orders are supported — you can combine different products in one shipment.

**Q: Can I order samples before placing a bulk order?**
A: Yes, samples are available for most products. Sample orders ship within 3–5 days via express courier. Sample cost is refundable against your first bulk order.

**Q: Do you support mixed orders?**
A: Absolutely. Our [mixed wholesale order service](/services/mixed-wholesale-orders/) lets you combine multiple SKUs and categories into one order with low MOQ per item.

## Pricing & Payment

**Q: What are your payment terms?**
A: For new customers, we require 30% deposit to start production and 70% balance before shipping. For repeat customers, net-15 or net-30 terms may be available after establishing a trading history.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: T/T (bank transfer), Western Union, and PayPal (for sample orders only).

**Q: Are prices negotiable for large orders?**
A: Yes, volume discounts apply for orders over USD 5,000. Contact us for a customized quote.

## Shipping & Delivery

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: It depends on the method:
- Express (DHL/FedEx/UPS): 3–7 days
- Air freight: 7–12 days
- Sea freight (LCL/FCL): 20–40 days

**Q: Do you handle customs clearance?**
A: We handle China export customs. Import customs in your country is your responsibility, though we provide all necessary documents (commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading).

**Q: Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?**
A: Yes, we offer FBA prep services including FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, and palletization.

## Products & Quality

**Q: Are your products certified?**
A: Many of our products carry CE, GS, or ANSI certifications. Certification details are listed on each product page. Custom certification can be arranged for large orders.

**Q: What if the products don't match the description?**
A: We offer pre-shipment [quality inspection](/services/quality-inspection/) and provide photo reports. If products arrive defective due to our error, we offer replacement or refund.

**Q: Can I customize products with my logo?**
A: Yes, we offer [OEM manufacturing](/services/oem-manufacturing/) including laser engraving, custom packaging, and product configuration changes.
