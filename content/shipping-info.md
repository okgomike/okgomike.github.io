# Shipping Information

## Shipping Methods

**ToolsD** offers three main shipping methods to fit your timeline and budget:

### Express Courier (3–7 Days)
- **Carriers:** DHL, FedEx, UPS
- **Best for:** Samples, urgent orders under 100 kg
- **Tracking:** Door-to-door with real-time online tracking
- **Note:** Higher per-kg cost, but fastest and most convenient

### Air Freight (7–12 Days)
- **Options:** Airport-to-airport or door-to-airport
- **Best for:** Medium orders (100–500 kg)
- **Tracking:** Air waybill (AWB) tracking
- **Note:** Good balance of speed and cost

### Sea Freight (20–40 Days)
- **Options:** LCL (Less than Container Load) or FCL (Full Container Load)
- **Best for:** Bulk orders over 500 kg or 2 CBM
- **Tracking:** Container number / Bill of Lading (BOL) tracking
- **Note:** Lowest per-kg cost, ideal for large-volume importers

## Customs & Documentation

### We Provide
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Bill of Lading / Air Waybill
- Certificate of Origin (if required)
- Export customs declaration (China side)

### Your Responsibility
- Import customs clearance in your country
- Import duties and taxes (if applicable)
- Any special import licenses or permits

## Packaging for Transit

All orders are packed to export standards:
- 5-layer corrugated cartons for sea freight
- Moisture-absorbent bags for humid destinations
- Fragile items wrapped in bubble foam
- Palletized shipments shrink-wrapped for stability

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive:
1. Tracking number within 24 hours
2. Carrier website link for real-time updates
3. Estimated delivery date
