# How It Works

Buying wholesale hardware and tools from **ToolsD** is straightforward. Here's exactly what happens from your first inquiry to delivery at your door.

## Step 1: Browse & Select Products

- Explore our [product catalog](/products/) organized by category
- Use the inquiry list button to save products you're interested in
- Check MOQ, price range, and specifications on each product page

## Step 2: Submit Your Inquiry

- Send your product list via [WhatsApp](https://wa.me/8613800138000) or [email](mailto:sales@toolsd.com)
- Include quantities, destination country, and any customization needs
- We typically respond within 12–24 hours

## Step 3: Receive Consolidated Quote

- We calculate product costs, packaging, and shipping as one package
- You'll see a clear breakdown: FOB price, shipping estimate, and total landed cost
- No hidden fees — what we quote is what you pay

## Step 4: Sample Order (Optional)

- Not sure about quality? Order samples first
- Samples ship within 3–5 days via DHL/FedEx/UPS
- Sample cost is refundable against your first bulk order

## Step 5: Confirm & Payment

- We send a Proforma Invoice (PI) with payment details
- Payment methods: T/T (bank transfer), Western Union, PayPal (for samples)
- 30% deposit to start production, 70% before shipping

## Step 6: Production & Inspection

- Standard lead time: 7–15 days for stocked items, 15–30 days for OEM orders
- Optional [quality inspection](/services/quality-inspection/) before shipment
- Photo report provided for all orders

## Step 7: Shipping & Delivery

- We handle China export customs declaration
- You receive tracking information within 24 hours of dispatch
- Delivery time depends on shipping method: express (3–7 days), air (7–12 days), sea (20–40 days)
