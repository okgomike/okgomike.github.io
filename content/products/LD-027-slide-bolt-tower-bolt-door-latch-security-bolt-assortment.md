# LD-027 Slide Bolt Tower Bolt Door Latch Security Bolt Assortment
## Product Details
This all-purpose tower slide bolt hardware set provides reliable physical locking security for household doors, garden gates, bathroom partitions and storage cabinets, with three material grades to match different usage intensity. Cost-effective die-cast zinc alloy is the standard option for indoor cabinet and light door use, while stainless steel and solid wrought iron versions deliver durable anti-rust performance for outdoor exposed gates.

Four decorative & protective surface finishes are optional: bright zinc white for plain storage cabinets, scratch-resistant black powder coat for modern matte decor, vintage brass tone for retro wooden doors, and mirror chrome plating for glossy high-end furniture. Every specification includes a matched strike plate and full set of mounting screws to skip extra accessory procurement during installation. An upgraded spring-loaded auto-lock mechanism can be customized for users needing one-way self-locking gate solutions.

Multiple retail and bulk packaging modes are supported, including single blister packs for offline hardware stores and unified color boxes with tailor-made foam inserts. Full OEM & ODM customization services cover body logo engraving, bespoke bolt lengths, exclusive surface coating formulas and custom spring latch structures, catering to global door hardware wholesalers and furniture component manufacturers.
