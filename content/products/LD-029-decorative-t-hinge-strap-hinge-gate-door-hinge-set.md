# LD-029 Decorative T-Hinge Strap Hinge Gate Door Hinge Set
## Product Details
This ornamental heavy-duty T-strap hinge series perfectly balances structural load-bearing performance and vintage decorative aesthetics, specially designed for farmhouse barn doors, outdoor garden gates, storage sheds and rustic wooden cabinet renovation projects. Three material options match different usage scenarios: lightweight cold rolled steel for indoor furniture, high-load cast iron for large heavy gates, and corrosion-resistant stainless steel for humid coastal outdoor environments.

Five decorative surface treatments are provided to fit diverse retro and modern decoration styles, including matte black/white powder coat, anti-rust zinc plating, vintage brass tone and antique bronze finish. All sizes from 4 inch to 12 inch adopt 2.0mm-4.0mm thick plates with pre-drilled universal mounting holes for convenient installation; two pin structures (fixed solid pin and detachable removable pin) are optional to meet door disassembly demands.

Multiple packaging solutions are supported, including single independent polybags and custom printed color retail boxes. Full OEM & ODM customization services are available, such as embossed brand logos on hinge straps, bespoke hinge dimensions, exclusive decorative surface coatings and tailor-made hole layouts, serving global door hardware wholesalers and wooden furniture manufacturers.
