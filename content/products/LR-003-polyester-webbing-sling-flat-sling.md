These certified polyester webbing slings provide safe efficient lifting of machinery pipes beams and construction materials in crane forklift and hoist applications. Reinforced loop eyes at both ends distribute lifting forces evenly while color-coded capacity stripes allow instant visual identification of safe working loads. The soft flexible webbing conforms to irregular loads without scratching painted or polished surfaces unlike chain or wire rope slings. Available in 1-ply 2-ply and 4-ply constructions with working load limits from 1 ton to 12 tons.
