This 12-inch professional hacksaw frame combines a lightweight die-cast aluminum body with adjustable blade tension for precise metal, plastic, and pipe cutting on job sites and in workshops. The front-mounted tension lever enables fast tool-free blade changes, a critical advantage for plumbers and metalworkers who switch between blade pitches throughout the workday.

Adjustable tension from 50N to 120N allows operators to fine-tune blade stiffness for different materials: lower tension for brittle blades on thin sheet metal, higher tension for heavy blades on pipe and thick stock. The 45-degree blade mounting option enables flush cutting in corners and against walls where 90-degree cutting is impossible. Spare blades store inside the hollow frame tube for safe, convenient access. Four standard blade pitch options from 18TPI to 32TPI cover every cutting application from rebar and exhaust pipe to delicate sheet metal. OEM frame colors and custom blade configurations support branded wholesale and distribution programs.
