This 12-inch professional hacksaw frame adopts lightweight die-cast aluminum construction paired with adjustable blade tension, delivering stable precise cutting performance for metal, plastic, pipe and rod on construction job sites and professional workshops. The front-mounted tension knob enables tool-free fast blade replacement, a huge convenience for plumbers and metal fabricators who frequently switch different tooth-pitch blades during daily work.

Tension range is fully adjustable from 50N up to 120N to match various workpiece materials: low tension protects thin brittle sheet metal blades from snapping, while high tension eliminates blade wandering when cutting thick pipes and solid bars. Dual mounting angles (45° & 90°) support flush trimming against walls and tight internal corners where straight vertical cutting cannot reach. The hollow frame tube provides built-in hidden storage for spare blades, keeping accessories intact during transport.

Four standard blade pitches (18TPI/24TPI/32TPI) cover all mainstream cutting scenarios, from thick construction rebar and exhaust tubing down to delicate thin metal sheets.
