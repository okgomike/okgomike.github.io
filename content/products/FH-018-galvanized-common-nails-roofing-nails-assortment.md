This galvanized common and roofing nail assortment supplies complete fastening solutions for timber framing, roof installation, fencing and general carpentry across construction and renovation projects. Our 40–60g/m² hot-dip galvanized coating delivers reliable 10-year anti-corrosion performance for outdoor exposed structures like fences and formwork.

Standard common nails ranging 1-inch to 6-inch fit all framing tasks from thin trim to heavy timber joints, while dedicated roofing nails with oversized flat heads stop asphalt shingles and roof felt from tearing or pulling loose. Sharp diamond tips pierce dense hardwood without pre-drilling, and smooth shanks accommodate natural wood expansion and contraction to avoid material splitting. Available in 1kg, 5kg and 25kg bulk packaging to cut unit packaging expenses and simplify on-site inventory management for large construction jobs.

We operate an in-house hot-dip galvanizing production line to guarantee consistent coating thickness across all batches. Premium copper and stainless steel variants are offered for high-end roofing projects requiring superior corrosion resistance. Custom box branding, bespoke nail dimensions and specialized tropical hardwood fastener designs are available for wholesale building material distributors.
