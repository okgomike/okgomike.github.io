These industrial tin snips and aviation snips feature precision-ground SK5 high-carbon steel blades with aggressive serrations that grip sheet metal during cutting to prevent slipping and ensure clean edges. Color-coded bi-material handles identify cut direction while the integrated spring and locking mechanism enable safe one-handed operation.
