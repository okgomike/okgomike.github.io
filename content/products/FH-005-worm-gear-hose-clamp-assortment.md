These worm gear hose clamps feature precision-rolled stainless steel or galvanized steel bands with machine-threaded worm screws that deliver uniform circumferential pressure for leak-free sealing of hoses pipes and tubing. The perforated band design allows the worm gear to engage smoothly without stripping while the rounded band edges prevent cutting into soft hose materials. Available in standard 8mm and 9mm widths for general automotive and plumbing use plus heavy-duty 12mm widths for industrial hydraulic and agricultural applications. Each size range is marked on the band for quick identification during installation. Essential for automotive repair shops plumbing contractors irrigation installers and manufacturing facilities that need reliable hose connections.
