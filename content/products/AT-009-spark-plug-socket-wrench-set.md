This spark plug socket wrench set provides everything needed for safe, efficient spark plug replacement in modern automotive engines. The chrome vanadium steel sockets feature thin-wall construction for access to recessed spark plugs in compact engine bays, and the internal rubber retention insert grips the ceramic insulator securely to prevent dropping plugs into deep wells.

Available in the four most common sizes: 14mm, 16mm, 18mm and 21mm to cover Japanese, European, American and motorcycle engines. The included extension bars and T-handle provide the reach and leverage needed for hard-to-access plugs, while the 3/8-inch drive fits standard ratchets and torque wrenches for precise installation torque.

The mirror-chrome finish resists corrosion and cleans easily after exposure to combustion deposits. Essential for auto repair shops, quick-lube centers, motorcycle service departments and DIY enthusiasts performing routine tune-ups and engine diagnostics.
