# LD-028 Self-Locking Hasp Lock Latch Padlock Hasp Assortment
## Product Details
This multi-scene security hasp series supplies two locking structures (spring self-locking & ordinary manual type) to meet furniture, storage and outdoor gate locking demands, fully compliant with QB/T domestic door hardware industry standards. Three raw material grades are optional: cost-effective cold rolled steel for indoor cabinets, corrosion-resistant stainless steel for damp outdoor sheds, and lightweight die-cast zinc alloy for small drawers and gift boxes.

All specifications cover 2.5 inch to 4.5 inch lengths with adjustable plate thickness from 1.2mm to 2.0mm to bear different locking loads. Four anti-rust and decorative surface treatments are supported: zinc white electroplating, matte black powder coat, anti-rust nickel plating and vintage brass tone plating. Each complete set is pre-equipped with matched staple and mounting screws, requiring no extra accessory purchase for installers.

Retail hanging blister packs and bulk polybag cartons are both available for offline stores and large project procurement. We provide full OEM & ODM customization service including embossed logo on hasp plate, bespoke size/thickness, exclusive coating colors and tailor-made spring self-locking mechanisms, serving global furniture hardware wholesalers and storage equipment manufacturers.
