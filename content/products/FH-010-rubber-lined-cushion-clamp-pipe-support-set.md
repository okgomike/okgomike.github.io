These rubber-lined cushion clamps and pipe supports provide vibration-dampened secure mounting for pipes hoses and cables in applications where noise reduction and surface protection are critical. The thick EPDM NBR or silicone rubber insert creates a cushioned barrier between the metal clamp body and the pipe preventing galvanic corrosion absorbing pump and flow-induced vibrations and eliminating rattling noise in HVAC automotive and industrial systems. The stamped steel band with precision-riveted rubber insert maintains consistent clamping pressure across temperature cycles from -40C to +150C. Available in carbon steel with corrosion-resistant finishes and 304 stainless steel for marine food processing and chemical environments. Color-coded rubber inserts allow instant visual identification of pipe contents and system types.
