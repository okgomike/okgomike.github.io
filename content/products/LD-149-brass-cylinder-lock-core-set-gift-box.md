  - question: "How do I measure my existing cylinder for replacement?"
    answer: "Remove your existing cylinder from the door. Measure the total length from edge to edge. Then measure from the outside edge to the center screw hole (external dimension) and from the inside edge to the center screw hole (internal dimension). The sum of these two measurements equals the total cylinder length needed."
  - question: "What is the difference between pin tumbler and leaf tumbler cylinders?"
    answer: "Pin tumbler cylinders use spring-loaded pins that are lifted to the shear line by key cuts. Leaf tumbler cylinders use thin steel plates (leaves) that are rotated by key cuts. Leaf tumblers provide significantly higher security because the leaf pattern is much harder to pick and the keys are more difficult to duplicate."
