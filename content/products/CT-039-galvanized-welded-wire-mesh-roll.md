This galvanized welded wire mesh roll provides heavy-duty fencing and reinforcement solutions for construction sites, animal enclosures, gardens, and industrial applications. The electro-welded joint process creates consistent fusion at every intersection, delivering uniform shear strength across the entire roll surface. The hot-dip galvanized coating at 40-80g/m2 provides 10 to 15 years of corrosion resistance in outdoor environments, while the optional PVC coating extends service life beyond 20 years and adds color options that blend with landscape and architectural surroundings.

Available in mesh openings from fine 25mm for poultry and small animal containment to coarse 150mm for large animal and perimeter fencing, with wire diameters from 1.5mm to 4.0mm to match every strength requirement. The selvedge edge option prevents wire unraveling during unrolling and installation, saving time and reducing material waste on job sites. Custom roll widths up to 2.0 meters and lengths up to 30 meters minimize on-site cutting and joining. Contractors, fencing installers, and agricultural operators benefit from the in-house galvanizing line that controls coating weight consistency across every batch.
