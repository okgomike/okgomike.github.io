This heavy-duty cast iron pipe wrench assortment covers every plumbing and HVAC pipe size from 6-inch to 24-inch models, providing contractors and maintenance teams with the precise tool for each job. The drop-forged steel jaws feature precision-ground teeth that bite into round pipe surfaces without slipping, even when coated in grease or pipe compound.
