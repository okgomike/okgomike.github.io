These marine-grade stainless steel padlocks are engineered for outdoor marine industrial and high-humidity environments where corrosion protection is critical. The 304 stainless steel body and shackle resist rust saltwater and chemical exposure while maintaining smooth key operation year after year. The precision pin tumbler or disc tumbler mechanism is protected by sealed chambers that keep moisture and debris out of the keyway. Mirror-polished and brushed satin finishes provide professional appearance on gates containers warehouse equipment and marine vessels. Available in sizes from 30mm for lockers to 70mm for industrial gates with matching key systems for facility-wide access control.
