These solid brass padlocks combine timeless durability with reliable pin tumbler security in sizes ranging from compact 20mm luggage locks to heavy-duty 70mm gate locks. The solid brass H59 body resists corrosion and tampering while the hardened steel shackle withstands cutting and prying attempts. Precision-machined pin tumbler cylinders with brass pins provide smooth key operation and pick resistance. Available in polished brass finish for classic appearance or nickel-plated brass for enhanced weather resistance. Each lock includes 2-3 keys and can be supplied in keyed-alike sets for convenience or keyed-different for individual security. Ideal for luggage school lockers storage cabinets gates toolboxes and indoor furniture.
