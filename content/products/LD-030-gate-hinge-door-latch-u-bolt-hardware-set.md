# LD-030 Gate Hinge Door Latch U-Bolt Hardware Set
## Product Details
This all-in-one outdoor gate hardware package integrates decorative strap hinges, self-locking slide latches and pipe fixing U-bolts, perfectly matching installation demands for garden fences, farm barn doors, storage sheds and rustic courtyard gates under QB/T domestic door hardware standards. Three material grades are supplied to fit different load and corrosion environments: lightweight cold rolled steel for small decorative gates, heavy solid cast iron for large heavy-load farm gates, and anti-rust stainless steel for coastal damp salt-air areas.

Four surface treatment solutions balance decorative appearance and outdoor anti-rust performance: matte black powder coat for retro farmhouse style, plain zinc plating for regular dry outdoor use, vintage brass tone for ornamental wooden gates, and hot dip galvanized finish for long-term exposure high-corrosion scenarios. Every full set is pre-equipped with matched mounting screws to skip separate accessory procurement during construction, supporting quick one-step installation.

Multiple retail and bulk packaging modes are supported including printed color gift boxes for offline hardware stores and loose polybag bulk cartons for large project procurement. Complete OEM & ODM customization services cover embossed brand logos on hinge plates, bespoke hinge lengths, exclusive surface coating formulas and custom hole layout patterns, serving global outdoor gate hardware wholesalers and wooden furniture manufacturers.
