# LR-007 Lifting Eye Hook Rigging Hook Safety Hook Set
## Product Details
This industrial heavy-duty lifting hook series is core safety rigging hardware for overhead cranes, warehouse hoists, shipping logistics and heavy machinery hoisting, fully certified by CE & GS and manufactured strictly to ASME B30.10 and EN 1677 international lifting safety standards. Two raw material grades match different load demands: cost-effective drop-forged Q235 carbon steel for light intermittent lifting tasks, and high-strength quenched 40Cr alloy steel for continuous heavy-load industrial crane operations with stable tensile performance.

The complete tonnage range covers 0.5 ton to 10 ton with dual safety factor options (4:1 / 5:1) to meet different regional construction safety codes. Four structural hook types are supported: fixed eye hook, 360° rotatable swivel hook, chain grab hook and spring self-locking safety latch hook to prevent accidental load falling. Multi protective surface treatments including three color powder coating and anti-rust hot dip galvanized finish extend service life for indoor and outdoor lifting environments.

Independent retail color boxes and bulk export cartons are both available for offline hardware stores and large engineering project procurement. Full OEM & ODM customization services cover printed brand logos on packaging, bespoke rated working load tonnage, exclusive paint colors and tailor-made swivel & safety latch mechanisms, serving global industrial rigging hardware wholesalers and crane equipment manufacturers.
