This flexible steel cable lock provides versatile security for bicycles, motorcycles, and personal property where rigid U-locks cannot fit. The 70# braided steel wire core delivers superior cut resistance and flexibility compared to twisted wire competitors, while the 1.5mm PVC coating prevents rust and protects bike frame finishes from scratches.

The self-coiling memory design allows the cable to spring back into a compact coil for neat storage on bike racks or in bags, eliminating the tangled mess of floppy cables. The lock head rotates 360 degrees to prevent cable kinking during locking and unlocking. Available in eight colors and five lengths from 600mm to 1500mm, this cable lock covers everything from single bike wheel locking to securing multiple bikes together through frames and wheels. The loop-end design enables lasso-style locking around large posts and railings that U-locks cannot encompass. OEM branding, custom cable lengths, and retractable automatic rewind options support wholesale and retail programs.
