These flexible endless round slings feature a continuous loop of high-tenacity polyester yarn encased in a woven protective sleeve that resists abrasion UV degradation and chemical exposure. The round profile distributes lifting forces evenly around the load circumference making them ideal for choke hitch and basket lifting configurations. Unlike chain or wire rope the soft polyester construction grips irregular shapes securely without marring polished painted or delicate surfaces. Color-coded capacity stripes correspond to EN 1492-2 standards for instant visual identification of safe working loads from 1 ton to 30 tons. Available in diameters from 25mm to 150mm with circumferences from 1 meter to 12 meters. Essential for rigging companies manufacturing plants shipyards and construction sites requiring versatile damage-free lifting solutions.
