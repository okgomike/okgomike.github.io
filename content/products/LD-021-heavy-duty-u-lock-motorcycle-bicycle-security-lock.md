This heavy duty U-lock delivers maximum security for motorcycles, bicycles, and electric scooters in urban parking environments. The 45# hardened steel shackle heat-treated to HRC 55-62 provides true cut resistance that standard mild steel U-locks cannot match, forcing thieves to resort to noisy angle grinders rather than quiet bolt cutters.

The double-locking mechanism secures both shackle legs independently, eliminating the single-point vulnerability that makes ordinary U-locks susceptible to pry bar attacks. The 7-pin anti-drill brass cylinder with hardened steel pins resists drilling, picking, and bumping techniques commonly used by thieves. A silicone dust cover seals the keyway against rain, dust, and road debris, extending cylinder life in outdoor exposure. The included mounting bracket with rubber lining attaches securely to round and oval frame tubes from 25mm to 40mm diameter, keeping the lock accessible during rides without rattling or frame damage. OEM branding, custom shackle clearances for fat bikes, and smart alarm/Bluetooth options support wholesale and retail distribution programs.
