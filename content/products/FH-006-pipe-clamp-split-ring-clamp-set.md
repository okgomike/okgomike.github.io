These heavy-duty pipe clamps and split ring supports provide secure vibration-dampened mounting for pipes tubes conduits and cables in commercial residential and industrial installations. The rubber-lined saddle prevents direct metal contact with the pipe eliminating galvanic corrosion and reducing noise transmission from fluid flow and pump vibration. Split-ring designs allow post-installation clamping without disconnecting the pipe while Munsen-ring and two-bolt styles provide rigid support for heavy-duty fire protection and HVAC systems. Available in carbon steel with galvanized or powder-coated finishes and 304 stainless steel for outdoor marine and food processing environments.
