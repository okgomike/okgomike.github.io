  - question: "What is the difference between swivel and fixed casters?"
    answer: "Swivel casters rotate 360 degrees for steering and maneuverability, typically used on two corners of a cart. Fixed casters roll only forward and backward, providing directional stability, typically used on the opposite two corners. Most four-wheel equipment uses two swivel and two fixed for balanced maneuverability."
  - question: "Can PU casters be used outdoors?"
    answer: "Standard PU casters are designed for indoor use on smooth floors. For outdoor or rough surface use, select casters with larger diameter wheels (150mm+) and thicker tread. Avoid prolonged UV exposure as it can degrade polyurethane over time."
