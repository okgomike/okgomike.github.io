This stainless steel door hinge assortment provides the complete range of sizes and load capacities needed for residential, commercial, and institutional door installations. The 304 stainless steel construction resists rust and corrosion for over 20 years in indoor environments, with the option of 201 stainless steel for cost-sensitive projects in dry climates.

The ball bearing mechanism replaces traditional plain washer designs, delivering silent, smooth door swing operation rated for 100,000 open/close cycles without wear or sagging. Four size options from 3-inch to 6-inch with three thickness grades cover door weights from 40kg to 120kg per pair. The removable pin design allows door detachment for maintenance or relocation without unscrewing the hinge from the frame. In-house CNC knuckle machining maintains 0.05mm tolerance for consistent leaf alignment and precise pin fit. OEM laser engraving, custom finishes, and exclusive hinge silhouettes support branded wholesale and distribution programs.
