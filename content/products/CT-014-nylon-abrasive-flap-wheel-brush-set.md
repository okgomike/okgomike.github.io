These nylon abrasive flap wheels and brushes use high-strength nylon fibers impregnated with silicon carbide or aluminum oxide abrasive grains to provide controlled cutting action for deburring blending and finishing. Unlike wire brushes they will not rust shed wires or contaminate stainless steel and aluminum workpieces. The flexible conformable design reaches into corners and complex profiles making them ideal for aerospace automotive marine and general metal fabrication applications. Available in mounted shank styles for die grinders and arbor-hole styles for bench grinders.
