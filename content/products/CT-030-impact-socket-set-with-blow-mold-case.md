These heavy-duty impact socket sets are engineered from chrome molybdenum steel with a black phosphate or black oxide finish that absorbs the extreme shock loads generated by impact wrenches without cracking or shattering. The 6-point design grips fastener flats to prevent rounding, while the heavy-duty wall thickness withstands sustained high-torque applications.

Deep socket models reach recessed nuts and bolts on suspension components and engine mounts. All sockets are clearly laser-etched with size markings for instant identification even in dim workshop conditions.

All pieces are stored in rugged blow-mold cases fitted with metal latches, capable of surviving drops and impacts on construction and repair job sites. Ideal for automotive technicians, truck mechanics, heavy equipment operators and industrial maintenance crews.
