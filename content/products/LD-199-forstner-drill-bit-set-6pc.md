  - question: "Can Forstner bits be used in a drill press?"
    answer: "Yes, Forstner bits perform exceptionally well in drill presses, providing precise depth control and consistent hole quality. Use lower speeds (400-800 RPM for 15-35mm bits, 200-400 RPM for 50mm bits) and secure the workpiece firmly."
  - question: "How do I prevent the bit from overheating?"
    answer: "Withdraw the bit periodically to clear chips from the cutting rim. Reduce drill speed for larger diameter bits. Apply moderate pressure without forcing. For deep holes, take multiple depth increments. Overheating dulls the HSS cutting edge and can burn the wood."
