These T-handle sliding socket wrenches combine the speed of a spinner with the torque capacity of a breaker bar. The chrome vanadium steel sockets with precision 6-point or 12-point broaching grip fastener flats without rounding corners, while the sliding cross-bar allows optimal positioning for maximum leverage in confined engine bays. The ergonomic TPR soft-grip handle reduces hand fatigue during extended use, and the detent ball ensures sockets stay securely engaged even when working overhead.

Available in 1/4", 3/8" and 1/2 inch drive sizes with comprehensive metric socket assortments from 8mm to 32mm. The sliding bar design permits one-handed fastener spinning when clearance allows and two-handed power when breaking loose stubborn bolts.

Essential for automotive technicians, motorcycle mechanics and agricultural equipment maintainers who need versatile, reliable socket drive tools.
