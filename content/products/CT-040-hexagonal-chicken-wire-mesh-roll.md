This hexagonal chicken wire mesh roll delivers flexible, lightweight fencing for poultry enclosures, garden protection, and decorative landscaping applications. The hexagonal weave pattern provides three-dimensional flexibility that conforms to irregular terrain, hills, and curved boundaries without the rigidity limitations of welded mesh. The double-twist construction at each intersection prevents the mesh from unraveling if individual wires break, maintaining enclosure integrity even after damage.

The PVC coating at 0.4-0.6mm thickness provides UV resistance and mechanical abrasion protection while adding color options that blend with garden and landscape environments. Even if the PVC coating is scratched or damaged, the hot-dip galvanized steel core continues to resist rust and corrosion. Available in mesh openings from 13mm for bird exclusion to 75mm for large animal containment, with wire diameters from 0.8mm to 2.0mm to match every strength and flexibility requirement. The triple-twist option provides enhanced strength for high-tension animal containment applications. Custom roll lengths up to 50 meters reduce on-site joining and waste. OEM branding, custom PVC colors, and specialty mesh designs support wholesale and agricultural distribution programs.
