This metal chain and spring hook hardware assortment provides versatile welded chain and snap hook combinations for pet leashes, plant hangers, key chains, bag straps, and light-duty securing applications. The welded steel chain construction maintains consistent strength across every link, unlike twisted chain alternatives that develop weak points where the twist is imperfect.

Each chain size is tested to its rated working load with a 4:1 safety factor, ensuring reliable performance in daily use. The spring-loaded snap hooks feature a safety latch that prevents accidental opening, a critical advantage over plain hooks that can release under vibration or unexpected loading. Six chain diameters from 3mm to 10mm and five hook sizes from 4mm to 12mm enable custom assembly for any application from delicate jewelry chains to robust pet tethering. Electro-galvanized coating provides indoor corrosion resistance, while hot-dip galvanized and stainless steel 304 options extend service life in outdoor, marine, and food processing environments. OEM branding, custom pre-assembled lengths, and decorative brass-plated collections support wholesale and retail distribution programs.
