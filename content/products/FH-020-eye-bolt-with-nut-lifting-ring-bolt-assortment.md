# FH-020 Eye Bolt with Nut Lifting Ring Bolt Assortment
## Product Details
This certified lifting eye bolt set is core rigging hardware for machinery hoisting, overhead suspension and industrial fixture securing, fully compliant with DIN 580 and BS 4278 international lifting standards. Two material grades are provided: cost-effective Q235 carbon steel with anti-rust zinc plating for regular construction, and corrosion-resistant 304 stainless steel for outdoor, marine and damp workshop environments.

All sizes from M6 to M16 adopt precision metric coarse threads, with conservative working load limits spanning 50kg to 500kg to guarantee lifting safety. Each complete kit comes pre-matched with a hex nut and flat washer, eliminating extra accessory procurement for installers. The integrated circular eye ring enables stable connection with wire ropes, slings and lifting straps for all rigging tasks.

Multiple packaging schemes are supported for retail and wholesale demand, including single blister packs and custom foam plastic boxes. We provide full OEM & ODM customization, such as custom thread length, eye ring diameter, private printed packaging and exclusive surface finishes, serving global lifting hardware wholesalers and machinery manufacturers.
