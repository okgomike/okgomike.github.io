These coil collated construction nails deliver superior holding power for timber framing, sheathing, pallet manufacturing, and decking applications where fastener withdrawal resistance is critical. The screw shank design provides 40% greater withdrawal resistance than smooth shank nails, making it the optimal choice for structural framing and engineered lumber connections that must withstand wind uplift and seismic forces.

The ring shank option offers a balanced combination of holding power and driveability for general construction where extreme withdrawal resistance is not required. Hot-dip galvanized coating provides corrosion protection for outdoor and pressure-treated lumber applications, resisting the ACQ chemicals that accelerate corrosion on uncoated fasteners. Chisel points reduce wood splitting during high-speed pneumatic driving, while diamond points penetrate dense hardwoods without pre-drilling. Available in wire-collated and plastic-collated configurations with 15-degree and 16-degree angles to match all major pneumatic coil nail gun brands. Custom collation angles, coil counts, and branded coil labels support wholesale and distribution programs.
