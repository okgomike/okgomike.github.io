# FH-022 Eye Bolt with Nut Lifting Ring Bolt Boxed Assortment
## Product Details
This certified lifting eye bolt boxed kit is core rigging hardware for machinery hoisting, overhead suspension and industrial fixture securing, fully compliant with DIN 580 and BS 4278 international lifting standards with CE & ISO9001 certification. Two material grades are provided: cost-effective Q235 carbon steel with anti-rust zinc plating for regular construction, and corrosion-resistant 304 stainless steel for outdoor, marine and damp workshop environments.

All sizes from M6 to M16 adopt precision metric coarse threads, with conservative working load limits spanning 50kg to 450kg to guarantee lifting safety. Each complete kit comes pre-matched with a hex nut and flat washer, and can be packed into custom printed plastic storage boxes for retail wholesale demand. The integrated circular eye ring enables stable connection with wire ropes, slings and lifting straps for all rigging tasks.

Multiple packaging schemes are supported for retail and bulk wholesale demand, including single blister packs, branded plastic storage boxes and bulk export cartons. We provide full OEM & ODM customization service such as private logo printed packaging, tailor-made thread length and eye ring diameter, exclusive surface finishes, serving global construction lifting hardware wholesalers and machinery manufacturers.
