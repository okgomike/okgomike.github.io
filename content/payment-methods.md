# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods

**ToolsD** accepts the following payment methods for wholesale orders:

### T/T (Bank Transfer) — Recommended
- **Best for:** All order sizes
- **Currency:** USD, EUR, GBP
- **Details:** Transfer to our company bank account
- **Processing time:** 2–5 business days
- **Fees:** Sender pays bank charges

### Western Union
- **Best for:** Small to medium orders under USD 3,000
- **Currency:** USD
- **Details:** Fast money transfer, no bank account needed
- **Processing time:** Same day
- **Fees:** Varies by sender country

### PayPal
- **Best for:** Sample orders only
- **Currency:** USD
- **Details:** Fast and secure online payment
- **Processing time:** Instant
- **Fees:** 4.5% service charge applies

## Payment Terms

### New Customers
- **30% deposit** to confirm order and start production
- **70% balance** before shipment
- Proforma Invoice (PI) provided with bank details

### Repeat Customers
After 3+ completed orders, we may offer:
- **Net-15** terms for orders under USD 5,000
- **Net-30** terms for established accounts
- Subject to credit review and trading history

## Payment Process

1. **Receive PI** — We send a Proforma Invoice with exact amounts and bank details
2. **Make Payment** — Send deposit via your chosen method
3. **Confirm Receipt** — We confirm payment and begin production/packing
4. **Balance Due** — We notify you when order is ready; pay balance before shipping
5. **Ship & Track** — Order ships within 24 hours of balance payment

## Important Notes

- All prices quoted are in **USD** unless otherwise specified
- Bank transfer fees are the **sender's responsibility**
- Orders are processed only after deposit is **confirmed received**
- We do not accept credit cards for wholesale orders
